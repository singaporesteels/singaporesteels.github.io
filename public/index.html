What We Offer

Ferrous Scrap

This scrap metals include steel and pig iron and alloys of iron with other metals (such as stainless steel). You can find ferrous metals everywhere in your house. The amount of iron in the scrap metal is the biggest difference between ferrous and non ferrous.

Non-Ferrous Scrap

The scrap metal which doesn't contain iron and has other metals including alloys, that does not contain iron in appreciable amounts. Important non-ferrous metals include aluminium, copper, lead, nickel, tin, titanium and zinc, and precisous metals
